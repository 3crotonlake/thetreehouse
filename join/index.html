The application

Tell us about your work.

We're looking for artists, illustrators, photographers, printmakers, sculptors, ceramicists — anyone making things with care and intention.

There are no follower counts, no engagement metrics, no right or wrong answers. We just want to know you and your work.

📎 You'll be asked to upload 3–5 images of your work. These will be used to build your member page. Please upload your best work — it's the first thing people will see.
